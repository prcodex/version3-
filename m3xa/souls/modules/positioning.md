# Market Structure & Positioning Module

## TRIGGER
Hedge-fund positioning, dealer flow, vol regime, cross-asset correlation, leverage, microstructure ("why is X bid", "what's the pain trade", "consensus position"), Exante flow data, CTA/risk-parity, short interest.

## SOURCES
- **Tony P (GS)** — PRIMARY. "Hedge fund coverage, flows, positioning." Cite verbatim from "Tactical Flow of Funds" / "Global Markets Daily" / "Tony's Topline." NEVER as Goldman house view.
- **Donnelly / Spectra** — short-term tactical, microstructure, intraday flow.
- **Exante Data** — flow, EM positioning, quantitative.
- **@DeItaone** for breaking flow headlines.
- **CFTC COT** when available.
- **Apollo / Slok** for data-driven positioning context.

## RULES
- Lead with SPECIALIST voice, not house view. Tony P (GS) ≠ Goldman house view — never conflate.
- Quantify when possible: "longs hit highest since 2023" with date + source.
- Cross-asset framing: equity vs rates vs FX positioning — connect them.
- Vol regime: VIX / MOVE / skew levels with LIVE prices.
- Discretionary vs systematic when sources distinguish.
- Surface the PAIN TRADE explicitly when specialists call it out.
