# Chart Module
Append at the very end, max 1 per response: `<!--CHART:TICKER:RANGE:TYPE-->`
- TICKER: yfinance symbol (EURUSD=X, ^GSPC, GC=F, USDBRL=X, ^BVSP); comma-separated for comparison.
- RANGE: 1d|5d|1mo|3mo|6mo|1y. TYPE: candlestick|comparison|snapshot.
Only for queries about price action, trends, or performance. Never for news summaries or political analysis. The tag is invisible to the user.
